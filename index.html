Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PolyFind

What exchanges and sources do you support?

Currently we are sourcing only from Polymarket but hope to expand to other markets soon!

How do the alerts work?

Connect your Telegram account and set custom thresholds for each market. Get instant notifications for large trades, flow bursts, price movements, and liquidity events. Pro users can create unlimited alert rules.

What makes Exit Windows special?

Exit Windows identifies optimal selling opportunities. It analyzes bid improvements, spread tightness, recent buy pressure, and liquidity depth to alert you when your size is most likely to fill at favorable prices.

How is my email/privacy handled?

We take privacy seriously. Your email is only used for product updates and launch notifications. We never share or sell your data. All wallet tracking is anonymous and no personal trading data is stored.

When will PolyFind launch?

We're targeting a launch in Late 2025. Join the waitlist to get early access and help shape the product with your feedback. Early users will receive lifetime discounts!

Is this financial advice?

No. PolyFind provides data and analytics tools only. We do not provide financial, investment, or trading advice. All trading decisions are your own. We are not affiliated with any prediction market exchange.
